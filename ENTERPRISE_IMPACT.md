### 5. DER Orchestrator (Renewable Integration)
**Distributed Energy Resource Coordination for 500GW Renewable Target**

**What:** Automatically balances solar/wind generation with feeder transmission constraints; prevents overload and curtailment losses.

**Why It's National-Changing:**
- **India's challenge:** Integrating 250GW+ renewables by 2028 within existing grid (4PM solar peak causes overload)
- **Current problem:** Grid curtails excess solar (9-15% wasted generation, ₹5,000+ crores annual loss)
- **SENTINEL solution:** Orchestrate solar + wind across feeders + batteries → 90%+ utilization
- **RDSS foundation:** DER orchestration is CORE to ₹3 lakh crore RDSS digitalization

**Real-World Scenario:**
```
Hour 14:00 (2PM peak solar):
- Solar plant: 50MW generated
- Feeder capacity: 40MW free
- SENTINEL action:
  1. Dispatch 40MW to grid
  2. Curtail 10MW gracefully (sell to ancillary market, not waste)
  3. Charge adjacent battery: 5MW [preventing curtailment escalation]
  
Result: 90% utilization vs. 70-80% manual dispatch
```

**Direct Impact:**
- **Curtailment loss reduction:** 9-15% → 2-3% (₹3,000-5,000 crores saved nationally)
- **Grid stability:** Frequency variation ±0.5Hz → ±0.1Hz (less load-shedding events)
- **RDSS alignment:** Direct support for "renewable integration" pillar of national initiative
- **Cost of renewable energy:** Prevented curtailment = lower LCOE, faster 500GW adoption

**Enterprise Features:**
- Real-time solar/wind forecasting (hour-ahead prediction accuracy 85%+)
- Battery placement optimizer (identifies 10 optimal locations for ₹10 crore battery storage)
- Demand response coordination (shift loads automatically when renewable surplus exists)
- Grid frequency support (battery discharge prevents under-frequency load shedding)
